About me

Hello, I'm Manuel Esteban Florez Lopez, an enthusiastic Software Developer from Cartagena de Indias, Colombia. With a focus on continuous learning and a future-oriented vision, I am ready to start my professional career. I stand out for my ability to integrate and contribute to creative teams, bringing not only technical competence but also great passion and a genuine desire to grow alongside a dynamic organization in the IT sector.
