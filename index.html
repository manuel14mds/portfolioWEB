About me

Hello, I'm Manuel Esteban Florez Lopez, an enthusiastic Software Developer from Cartagena de Indias, Colombia. As a graduate in Systems Engineering, I have specialized in the development and leadership of software projects, currently contributing my skills to a foundation. While still seeking my first formal IT employment experience, my journey combines solid academic foundations with practical experience in significant projects, demonstrating my readiness to tackle challenges in the dynamic world of technology.

With a focus on continuous learning and a future-oriented vision, I am ready to start my professional career. I stand out for my ability to integrate and contribute to creative teams, bringing not only technical competence but also great passion and a genuine desire to grow alongside a dynamic organization in the IT sector.
